PWG April, 2008 F2F Meeting

 

I. When/Where

The PWG F2F meeting will be held on April 16-18, 2008 in Phoenix AZ.

 

Meeting Venue Information:

Courtyard Phoenix Mesa
1221 South Westwood
Mesa, AZ 852101


The IEEE P2600 working group will be meeting at the same location on
April 14-15.


II. Where to stay
A block of rooms has been reserved at the Courtyard Phoenix Mesa. 
Details will of how to reserve the room, room rates and a link for the
meeting registration page will be posted by the end of February. 

Note: The Printer Working Group has signed a contract that
contains a room occupancy committment at that hotel. It is very important
that meeting attendees also stay at the hotel.

Please use the following link to make your hotel reservations:

http://cwp.marriott.com/phxme/ieeeisto/

The reservation deadline is March 21, 2008.

There will be a $65.00 per day meeting ($75.00 on-site) fee for this meeting.

Registration link:

http://pwg.isto.org/pwg_April08_reg.html
