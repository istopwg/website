I. When/Where

The joint PWG-OpenPrinting meetings will be held on May 14-17, hosted by Apple at their facilities in Cupertino, CA:

10500 N. De Anza Blvd.
Cupertino, CA 95014
[map]

A light breakfast and lunch will be served each day.

All sessions will be held in the "Beatles" conference room in De Anza 3. It is located just past the vistor's desk in the lobby.

II. Registration/RSVP

There is no registration fee for this meeting.

Please respond to the PWG May 2013 face-to-face meeting survey whether you are planning to attend the meeting in person, by phone, or not at all. This will help us plan for services and make any necessary reservations.
