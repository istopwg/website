III. Meeting Registration

 

There will be a $20 per day meeting fee for this meeting to cover catering.

This fee will be collected by the ISTO and will be managed in the same way as the

meeting fees for other previous PWG Face to Face meetings. Registration is required for this meeting.

If you are attending both the FSG and PWG meetings please register for both at the same time.

You do not need to register at both the PWG and FSG sites.

The following hotels are some of the options available while staying in Lexington.

 

Lexmark has negotiated rates that may be available but only if you make arrangements through Lexmark's travel

coordinator WorldTravel (aka BCD Travel). You can make arrangements by one of the following methods.

 

  1. Preferred method..call toll free: 1-800-944-4360 (option 3)

 

  1. Alternate metho..send an email to lexmark@bcdtravel.com with your request that includes: Name, Dates of Stay, Preferred hotel(s), Contact information , name of Lexmark contact (Jerry Thrasher), name of meeting you will be attending (PWG and/or FSG) and most importantly..your method of payment (credit card details).

 

You must mention both the meeting you are attending (PWG and/or FSG) and the Lexmark contact name (Jerry Thrasher) to get the negotiated rate.

V. Other items of note for travel to Lexington.

 

The local airport in Lexington KY is the Lexington Bluegrass Airport (LEX).

Other airports that may be options are the Cincinnati Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG)

or the Louisville International Airport (SDF).

Both of these airports are a 1.5 to 2 hour drive from Lexington.

 

Taxi service and public transportation in Lexington are marginal at bes..some hotels provide bus service to/from the airport

and to/from the Lexmark site, however a rental car is probably the best option.
