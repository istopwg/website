PWG December, 2007 F2F Meeting

 

I. When/Where

The PWG F2F meeting will be held on December 11-12, 2007 in Austin, TX. hosted by Dell.

 

Meeting Venue Information:
Building 3 (Parmer South 3)
701 E. Parmer Lane
Austin, TX 78753

The meetings will be held both days in the Dell Technical Briefing Center
(TBC) in the Parmer South campus. (The Parmer South campus, NOT the Round
Rock campus.)

  • The Parmer South campus is about 20 miles north of the airport, about 12 miles north of the center of town. Parmer Lane crosses interstate I-35. It is also designated as Rt. 734 on some road signs.
  • The TBC itself is in the link between the S.2 (PS2) and S.3 (PS3) buildings, shown on the detail map.
  • There is visitor parking directly in front of the link between the two buildings.

An area MAP can be found at:

http://pwg-wiki.wikispaces.com/DellAustinMapsAndDirections
