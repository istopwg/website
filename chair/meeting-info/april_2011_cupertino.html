I. When/Where

The PWG meetings will be held on April 4-6, hosted by Apple at their facilities in Cupertino, CA:

1 Infinite Loop
Cupertino, CA 95014
[map]

All sessions will be at the Worldwide Developer Briefing Center. It is located to the left of the main entrance to building 1. Park in the visitor parking in front of building 1 (across from the Company Store) or in the employee parking lot (across from the Briefing Center) and enter through the Briefing Center doors.

A light breakfast and lunch will be served each day.

II. Registration/RSVP

There is no registration fee for this meeting.

Please respond to the PWG April 2011 face-to-face meeting survey whether you are planning to attend the meeting in person, by phone, or not at all. This will help us plan for services and make any necessary reservations.
