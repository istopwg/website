I. When/Where

The PWG meetings will be held on February 7-9, 2012, hosted by Canon at their facilities in Irvine, CA:

15975 Alton Parkway
Irvine, CA 92618-3731

The PWG meetings will be held in the “CDA Building”. Please park in the visitor spaces if possible. Enter at the front door marked with a green triangle and sign in at the reception desk. The meeting room is called Hollywood and is in the hall to the right as you come in.

Our organization name has changed from “Canon Development Americas” to “Canon USA R&D Center” but the maps don’t show this change. The “CDA Building” front doors are now marked with “Canon Business Solutions” and “Canon USA R&D Center”.

Canon will provide a continental breakfast, lunch, and afternoon snacks.

II. Registration/RSVP

There is no meeting fee for this meeting.

Please respond to the PWG February 2012 face-to-face meeting survey whether you are planning to attend the meeting in person, by phone, or not at all. This will help us plan for network services and to make badges for the attendees.
