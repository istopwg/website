HOTEL ACCOMMODATIONS:

 

For your convenience, we have arranged a sleeping room block at the Embassy Suites Piscataway-Somerset.

The room rate is $115.00 per night (plus 14% tax) for single or double occupancy. For this meeting, PWG is *not* committed to filling the room block, so there is no penalty to PWG if you choose to select another local hotel (a few options are listed below my email signature). However, Embassy Suites offers complimentary wireless high-speed internet access; full cooked-to-order breakfast; and nightly Manager's Reception with your two-room suite. Also, Embassy Suites provides complimentary shuttle service between the hotel and IEEE.

 

The room block will be available beginning Friday, March 16.

Please make your hotel reservations by calling the Embassy Suites at +1-732-980-0500 and requesting the PWG (or IEEE-ISTO) rate.

You may also email your reservation to <terri@andrews@hilton.com>.

Availability is guaranteed if you reserve your sleeping room by Monday, April 2.
